Research Interests

  • The Cryptyc Project: foundations of computer security; type systems for secrecy and authenticity.

  • The Temporal Aspects Project: formal basis of aspect-oriented programming; aspects for security.

  • The Full Abstraction Factory: mathematical foundations of programming languages; scalable models of object-based component systems.
