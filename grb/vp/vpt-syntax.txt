
Timed value-passing CCS extends the language for value-passing CCS.
The syntax of the new constructs is:

  $0		timed zero
  $a.P		delayed action prefix; 'a' an action that can be
                indexed and/or parametrized
  i.P		timed prefix; 'i' a NUM
  <e>.P		timed prefix; 'e' a postive integer expression
  P ++ Q	strong plus
  ssum(x:e,P)   strong summation; 'e' a set expression
