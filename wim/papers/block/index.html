Fast Block Noncoherent Decoding


Wim Sweldens




Abstract: We present a fast algorithm for exact maximum likelihood multi-symbol noncoherent PSK decoding. While the standard algorithm is exponential in the rate and the block length, our algorithm is rate independent and linear-logarithmic in the block length.
